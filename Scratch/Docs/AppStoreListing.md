## App Store Description (4,000 chars max)

Scratch is the AI golf coach in your pocket — smarter than a swing analyzer, cheaper than a lesson.

Whether you're trying to break 100, shoot in the 80s, or reach single digits, Scratch gives you a clear answer to the one question every golfer asks:

**What should I do next to lower my handicap?**

---

**YOUR PERSONALIZED ROADMAP**
Set your goal — Break 100, Break 90, Break 80, Single Digit, or Scratch — and get an instant improvement roadmap built around your game. Tell us your biggest weakness and we'll focus your coaching there first.

**AI GOLF COACH**
Ask your coach anything. Get specific advice, not vague tips. "What should I practice this week?" "How do I stop 3-putting?" "Build me a 45-minute range session." Your coach uses your round history and swing analysis to give answers that actually fit your game.

**SWING ANALYSIS**
Upload a video from the face-on or down-the-line angle. Your AI coach identifies the one issue hurting your scores most, gives you one drill to fix it, and tells you how much it could be worth.

**SCORE + HANDICAP TRACKING**
Log a round in under 30 seconds: score, what cost you the most strokes, done. No mandatory stats. No data homework. Your handicap trend updates automatically.

**WEEKLY PRACTICE PLANS**
Stop hitting balls hoping something changes. Every week, Scratch builds a focused practice plan targeting the specific part of your game that will move your handicap fastest.

**PROGRESS & MILESTONES**
Track your handicap trend over time. See your stroke leaker breakdown. Unlock milestones as you hit goals — Break 90, Break 80, Single Digit, Scratch.

## Common Rejection Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| AI medical/health claims | Avoid words like "improve your physical health." Focus on "lower your score." |
| Swing analysis overreach | Use hedged language: "may," "likely," "based on this angle" |
| No Restore Purchases button | Already included in PaywallView |
| Subscription metadata incomplete | Complete all fields in App Store Connect before submission |
| Missing privacy policy | Must be a live URL before submission |
| Crash on launch | Test on real device, not just simulator |
| Missing camera permission string | Already defined — confirm in Info.plist |
| Subscription not working in Sandbox | Test the full purchase flow before submission |
| App is mostly a web view | App is native SwiftUI — no risk |
| Too similar to competitor | Differentiate: "AI coach" not "swing analyzer" |
